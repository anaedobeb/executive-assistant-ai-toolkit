# AI Prompts for Executive Support

## Meeting Summary Prompt

Summarize this meeting into:

* key decisions
* action items
* deadlines
* follow-ups
* risks/blockers

## Scheduling Prompt

Draft a professional scheduling response for an executive with limited availability across multiple time zones.

## Travel Planning Prompt

Create a business travel itinerary including:

* flights
* hotel
* meeting schedule
* transportation
* contingency planning

## Follow-Up Prompt

Write a concise follow-up message after a stakeholder meeting while maintaining a warm and professional tone.
